8-bit Flash ADC 3 Arduino C, LTSpice, Matlab, Circuit Analysis Signals and Systems (Olin Course)

Discretizing an Audio Signal with an Arduino

For our final project in Olin's Signals and Systems course, we implemented an 8-bit Analog to Digital Converter (ADC). The basic principle behind a simple Flash ADC is to compare a signal against the values at different nodes in a resistor ladder using comparators. By doing so, one can get a discretized version of the magnitude of the input signal. We used an Arduino Uno to read this data, and using a serial connection, we viewed the data in Matlab. The final write-up for the project can be fouund here!

Simple Battery Charger 3 Arduino C, Circuit Analysis Power Supplies (Olin Seminar)

A Lesson in Battery Charger Design

In the pilot version of Olin College's Power Supplies seminar, we learned about circuit-level design of various forms of voltage regulators ranging from standard linear, buck, and boost regulators to more obscure architectures like the Joule Thief. For our final project, we opted to use a buck regulator in constructing a simple battery charger.

The charger which we implemented was efectively a variable buck regulator whose DCM signal was set based on the charging state of the battery. If the battery was under its nominal charge, then the buck regulator was used in constant current mode. If the battery was at its nominal charge, then the regulator was used in constant voltage mode. We used an Arduino Uno to detect the battery's charge state and to control the DCM signal governing the output of the buck regulator. For more information, you can read our final write-up here!

RC Snake 5 Solidworks, Mechanical Design Design Nature (Olin Course)

A Bio-inspired Toy for Fourth Graders

In Design Nature, we were tasked with creating a game for fourth graders which involved a bio-inspired toy. As a member of Team Snake, we designed a game where the children were divided into two teams. One team controlled four small chicks using which were moved using magnets attached to rods while the other team piloted a remote-controlled snake. The children controlling the chicks attempted to avoid the snake while the other children tried to eat the chicks.
